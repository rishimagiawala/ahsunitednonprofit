Our Purpose

The goal of UNP is to facilitate and encourage the development of ideas that will benefit the community such as non-profits. One way that we will approach this is by sharing documentation and resources that have helped our respective non-profits come to fruition 

  • Self-Innovation -  We want students to either join the existing non-profits that are already established or create their own idea - something that they are passionate about.
