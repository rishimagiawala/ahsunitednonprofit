Our Purpose

The goal of UNP is to facilitate and encourage the development of ideas that will benefit the community such as non-profits. One way that we will approach this is by sharing documentation and resources that have helped our respective non-profits come to fruition

READ MORE

Self Innovation

We want students to either join the existing non-profits that are already established or create their own idea - something that they are passionate about. 

Engagement

Our club will work to engage our members in idea development and we will provide the resources and concepts necessary to bring their ideas to life

Flexibility

We want to be flexible with our memebers when it comes to meeting deadlines as we understand, that each person will have different timelines for their projects

FAQ

Are there club fees?

Our club requires no fees to join, as we already have the resources and instructional materials necessary to bring your idea to life!

How often do you meet?

We are planing on having a meeting once every 1-2 weeks.

Why should I join?

This club is useful to those who have always had good ideas, but they never knew how to bring them to fruition.
